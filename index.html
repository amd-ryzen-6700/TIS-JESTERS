Who We Are

About Us

“

TIS Phantom Shadow had a demanding and unforgettable 2025 season, earning the opportunity to compete at both Regionals and Nationals through months of hard work, innovation, and teamwork. Reaching those competitions was a major achievement, but the journey was far from easy. At both events, the team faced setbacks — the robot did not always perform as planned, the pressure was high, and unexpected problems arose at critical moments. These challenges were frustrating, but they pushed the team to think deeper, adapt faster, and support one another through difficult situations. Rather than viewing those moments as defeats, TIS Phantom Shadow chose to treat them as valuable lessons. The 2025 season revealed important areas for improvement in preparation, strategy, and execution. With renewed motivation and a clearer vision, TIS Phantom Shadow has decided to return in 2026 stronger than ever, ready to learn from the past, sharpen their skills, and strive for greater success together.
