Our Mission

We are the TIS Jesters, a dynamic First Lego League team dedicated to innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. Through robotics, we learn to solve real-world problems while building lasting friendships and developing essential skills for the future.

Our Robot

Our robot, "JesterBot", is the culmination of countless hours of design, testing, and refinement. Built with precision engineering and innovative attachments, JesterBot can navigate complex courses and complete challenging missions with remarkable accuracy. Its modular design allows for quick adaptations between competition rounds.
